# Information Security Policy

## Purpose

This policy sets BrightBox Retail Ltd's overall approach to protecting company, customer, supplier, and employee information.

## Scope

This policy applies to all staff, contractors, suppliers, systems, devices, and information used for BrightBox business.

## Policy requirements

- Company information must be protected according to its sensitivity and business value.
- Customer and employee information must be handled with care and only used for approved business purposes.
- Security responsibilities must be assigned to named business roles.
- Security risks must be recorded, reviewed, and treated in a proportionate way.
- Supporting policies must be followed for access control, passwords, MFA, data handling, backups, suppliers, remote working, incidents, and awareness.
- Security decisions should be practical for a small business and reviewed when risk or business needs change.

## Roles and responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Director | Approves this policy, sets priorities, and accepts residual risk. |
| Operations Manager | Maintains policy records and coordinates reviews. |
| IT Support Provider | Advises on technical controls and supports implementation. |
| Staff | Follow the policy and report security concerns. |

## Evidence or records

- approved policy pack
- management review notes
- risk register
- policy acknowledgement records

## Review frequency

This policy should be reviewed annually or after a major security incident or business change.

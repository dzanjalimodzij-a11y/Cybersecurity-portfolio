# Data Classification and Handling Policy

## Purpose

This policy sets simple rules for classifying, storing, sharing, and disposing of BrightBox information.

## Scope

This policy applies to all company, customer, supplier, and employee information.

## Classification levels

| Level | Examples | Handling rules |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| Public | Published website content, public product information | May be shared externally once approved. |
| Internal | Internal procedures, general team updates | Store in company cloud services; do not publish externally. |
| Confidential | Customer records, supplier terms, finance records | Restrict access to business need; share only through approved systems. |
| Restricted | Payroll data, admin credentials, incident details | Limit to named users; store securely; review access regularly. |

## Policy requirements

- Staff must consider the classification before storing or sharing information.
- Customer and employee data must be stored in approved business systems.
- Confidential and Restricted information must not be stored in personal email or personal cloud storage.
- External sharing must be limited to approved recipients and business need.
- Files should not be shared using open public links.
- Unneeded personal data should be deleted according to business and legal retention needs.
- Paper records containing Confidential or Restricted information must be disposed of securely.

## Roles and responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Operations Manager | Owns data handling guidance and reviews issues. |
| IT Support Provider | Supports secure storage and sharing settings. |
| Staff | Classify and handle data correctly. |

## Evidence or records

- data handling guidance
- file sharing review
- retention and deletion record
- disposal record where relevant

## Review frequency

This policy should be reviewed annually.

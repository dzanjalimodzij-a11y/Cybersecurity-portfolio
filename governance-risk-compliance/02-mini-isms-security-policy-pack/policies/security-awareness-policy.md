# Security Awareness Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines the security awareness activities staff need to complete.

## Scope

This policy applies to all BrightBox staff and contractors with access to company systems or data.

## Policy requirements

- New starters must receive basic security guidance during induction.
- Staff must understand how to report suspicious emails and security incidents.
- Staff must receive phishing awareness guidance.
- Staff must understand password and MFA expectations.
- Staff handling customer or employee data must receive data handling guidance.
- Annual refresher training should be completed by all staff.
- Awareness content should be short and relevant to the way BrightBox works.

## Roles and responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Operations Manager | Coordinates induction, refresher training, and completion records. |
| IT Support Provider | Provides technical input on current threats and reporting routes. |
| Staff | Complete required training and report suspicious activity. |

## Evidence or records

- induction checklist
- annual awareness completion record
- phishing guidance
- training attendance record

## Review frequency

This policy should be reviewed annually.

# Risk Register

| Risk ID | Risk description | Affected asset/process | Likelihood | Impact | Overall rating | Existing control | Recommended treatment | Owner |
|---------|------------------|------------------------|------------|--------|----------------|------------------|-----------------------|-------|
| BB-R01 | Phishing email leads to account compromise | Microsoft 365, shared email | Likely | High | High | Basic email filtering | Enforce MFA, train staff, improve reporting route | Operations Manager |
| BB-R02 | Leaver account remains active after staff departure | Microsoft 365, Shopify, cloud storage | Possible | High | High | Informal leaver process | Use leaver checklist and quarterly access reviews | Operations Manager |
| BB-R03 | Shared mailbox access is not controlled | Shared business email accounts | Possible | Medium | Medium | Shared mailboxes in use | Assign owners and review access quarterly | Operations Manager |
| BB-R04 | Lost or stolen laptop exposes business data | Staff laptops | Possible | High | High | Windows login required | Confirm encryption, screen lock, and reporting process | IT Support Provider |
| BB-R05 | Customer data is overshared in cloud storage | Cloud file storage, customer records | Possible | High | High | Cloud storage permissions | Apply data handling rules and review sharing links | Operations Manager |
| BB-R06 | Backup cannot be restored after ransomware or deletion | Backup service, Microsoft 365 | Possible | High | High | Backup service planned or partially configured | Document backup scope and complete restore tests | IT Support Provider |
| BB-R07 | Supplier with access to data has weak security controls | Suppliers, Shopify integrations | Possible | Medium | Medium | Supplier records exist | Complete supplier review and limit supplier access | Operations Manager |
| BB-R08 | Staff do not know how to report a security incident | Incident response process | Likely | Medium | High | Informal reporting | Issue incident response policy and keep incident log | Operations Manager |

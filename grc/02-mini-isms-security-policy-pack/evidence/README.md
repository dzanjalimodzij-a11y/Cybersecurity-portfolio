# Evidence

This folder contains example evidence records for the Mini ISMS policy pack. The records show how a small company could collect practical evidence that policies are being followed.

All records in this folder are simulated evidence created for this portfolio project. They are not real operational records, system screenshots, tickets, or signed documents.

## Evidence index

| Evidence ID | Evidence file | Related policy/register | What it demonstrates | Why it matters |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------------|----------------------|----------------|
| EV-001 | [Policy acknowledgement record](./EV-001-policy-acknowledgement-record.md) | Policy Register | Staff have acknowledged the policy pack. | Helps show that staff were told about the rules they are expected to follow. |
| EV-002 | [Access review record](./EV-002-access-review-record.md) | Access Control Policy | User access was reviewed across key systems. | Reduces the chance of old, excessive, or inappropriate access staying active. |
| EV-003 | [Backup restore test record](./EV-003-backup-restore-test-record.md) | Backup Policy | A backup restore was tested and recorded. | Confirms that backups can support recovery, not just exist on paper. |
| EV-004 | [MFA configuration record](./EV-004-mfa-configuration-record.md) | Password and MFA Policy | MFA coverage and exceptions were reviewed. | Supports control over account takeover risk. |
| EV-005 | [Incident log example](./EV-005-incident-log-example.md) | Incident Response Policy | A low-risk security incident was recorded and handled. | Shows that staff know how to report issues and that follow-up happens. |
| EV-006 | [Supplier security review](./EV-006-supplier-security-review.md) | Supplier Security Policy | A supplier was checked for basic security expectations. | Helps manage third-party risk. |
| EV-007 | [Security awareness training record](./EV-007-security-awareness-training-record.md) | Security Awareness Policy | Staff training completion was recorded. | Shows that awareness activity is tracked. |
| EV-008 | [Leaver checklist example](./EV-008-leaver-checklist-example.md) | Access Control Policy | Leaver access and device return checks were completed. | Reduces risk from unused accounts and missing assets. |
| EV-009 | [Policy exception record](./EV-009-policy-exception-record.md) | Policy Framework | A temporary exception was approved and time-limited. | Keeps exceptions visible and risk-managed. |
| EV-010 | [Management review notes](./EV-010-management-review-notes.md) | Governance Structure | Management reviewed risks, actions, and policy updates. | Shows that security governance is being reviewed, not left static. |

In a real business, evidence would include screenshots, exports, signed acknowledgements, logs, tickets, meeting notes, and system configuration records. For this portfolio project, the files are written as example records to show what that evidence could look like.

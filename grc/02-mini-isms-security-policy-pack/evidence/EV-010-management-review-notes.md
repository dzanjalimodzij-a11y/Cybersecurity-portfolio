Management review notes

Management review and improvement planning

| Field | Example entry |
|-------|---------------|
| Meeting date | 2026-06-28 |
| Attendees | Director, Operations Manager, IT Support Provider |

| Actions agreed | Complete Shopify MFA exception, close cloud sharing action, schedule next restore test |
| Next review date | 2026-09-30 |

## Why this evidence is important

Management review notes show that security is being discussed, decisions are recorded, and actions are assigned. This keeps the policy pack active rather than static.

## Review or collection frequency

Collect at least annually, with quarterly reviews for open risks and actions.

## Notes on limitations

Meeting notes should be supported by action tracking. Without follow-up, review notes do not prove that improvements were completed.

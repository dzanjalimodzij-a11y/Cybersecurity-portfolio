## Why this evidence is important

Signed or recorded acknowledgement helps show that staff were given the policy pack and understood that the rules apply to their work. It also gives the operations manager a simple way to chase missing acknowledgements.

## Review or collection frequency

Collect during induction, after major policy updates, and annually for the core policy pack.

## Notes on limitations

Acknowledgement does not prove that staff follow the policies. It should be supported by training, reminders, access reviews, and incident reporting.

# EV-009 - Policy Exception Record

## Evidence ID

EV-009

## Evidence title

Temporary policy exception record

## Related policy or register

[Policy Framework](../docs/04-policy-framework.md)

## Control area

Policy exceptions and risk ownership

## Example record details

| Field | Example entry |
|-------|---------------|
| Exception request | Temporary supplier admin access to cloud storage migration folder |
| Policy affected | Access Control Policy |
| Reason | Supplier needs short-term access to migrate approved business files |
| Risk | Supplier account could access files beyond the migration need if permissions are not restricted |
| Approval role | Director |
| Expiry date | 2026-07-05 |
| Compensating control | Access limited to one folder, MFA required, activity reviewed after migration |
| Owner | Operations Manager |

## Why this evidence is important

Exception records stop temporary workarounds becoming permanent. They show who accepted the risk, what controls were used, and when the exception should end.

## Review or collection frequency

Record when exceptions are approved and review before the expiry date.

## Notes on limitations

Exceptions should be rare. Repeated exceptions may mean the policy or process needs updating.

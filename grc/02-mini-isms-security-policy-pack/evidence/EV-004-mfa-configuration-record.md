## Why this evidence is important

MFA evidence helps show that the company has taken practical steps to reduce account takeover risk. It also makes exceptions visible so they can be followed up.

## Review or collection frequency

Review quarterly and whenever a new business system is introduced.

## Notes on limitations

This record does not replace a system export or screenshot. In a live environment, it should be supported by configuration evidence from each system.

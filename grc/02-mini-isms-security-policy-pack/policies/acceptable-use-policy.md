# Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines acceptable use of BrightBox devices, email, internet access, cloud services, and removable media.

## Scope

This policy applies to all staff and contractors using company systems or handling company information.

## Policy requirements

- Company devices and accounts must be used for approved business work.
- Staff must not install unauthorised software or browser extensions.
- Business email must not be used for personal sign-ups or non-business activity.
- Internet use must not expose the company to malware, illegal content, or reputational harm.
- Company data must be stored in approved cloud storage, not personal accounts.
- Removable media should only be used when approved by the operations manager.
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported immediately.
- Shared mailboxes must be used for business functions only and monitored by assigned owners.

## Prohibited activities

- sharing passwords
- bypassing security controls
- storing customer data in personal email or storage
- using unlicensed or pirated software
- connecting unknown USB devices
- sending offensive, unlawful, or misleading messages using company systems

## Roles and responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Operations Manager | Communicates acceptable use rules and records issues. |
| IT Support Provider | Supports device controls and software restrictions. |
| Staff | Use systems responsibly and report misuse or loss. |

## Evidence or records

- staff acknowledgement
- device inventory
- incident or misuse records
- lost device reports

## Review frequency

This policy should be reviewed annually.

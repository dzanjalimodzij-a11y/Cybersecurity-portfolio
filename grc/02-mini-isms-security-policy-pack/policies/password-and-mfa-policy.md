# Password and MFA Policy

## Purpose

This policy reduces the risk of account compromise by setting password and multi-factor authentication (MFA) requirements.

## Scope

This policy applies to Microsoft 365, Shopify, finance systems, cloud storage, admin accounts, supplier accounts, and any system used for BrightBox work.

## Policy requirements

- MFA must be enabled for Microsoft 365, Shopify, admin accounts, finance systems, and cloud services.
- Staff should use a password manager to create and store strong unique passwords.
- Passwords must not be reused between business and personal accounts.
- Passwords must not be shared by email, chat, spreadsheet, or written notes.
- Default passwords must be changed before systems or devices are used.
- Admin accounts must have stronger protection and must not be used for day-to-day email.
- Account recovery options must be kept up to date.
- Suspected compromised passwords must be reported immediately and reset with IT support.

## Roles and responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| IT Support Provider | Configures MFA, supports password resets, and reviews account settings. |
| Operations Manager | Tracks staff awareness and follows up on exceptions. |
| Staff | Use unique passwords, protect MFA methods, and report compromise. |

## Evidence or records

- MFA configuration record
- password manager guidance
- account recovery review
- compromised account record

## Review frequency

This policy should be reviewed annually or after any account compromise.

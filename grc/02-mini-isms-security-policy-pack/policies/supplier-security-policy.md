# Supplier Security Policy

## Purpose

This policy sets security expectations for suppliers that access BrightBox systems, process company data, or support key services.

## Scope

This policy applies to IT support, Shopify-related providers, payment providers, delivery and fulfilment partners, marketing platforms, finance systems, and other suppliers handling BrightBox data.

## Policy requirements

- Critical suppliers must be recorded and assigned a business owner.
- Supplier due diligence should be completed before a supplier handles Confidential or Restricted data.
- Supplier access must be limited to the agreed purpose.
- Supplier accounts must use named users where possible and MFA where available.
- Contracts or service terms should cover confidentiality, data handling, incident reporting, and service availability where relevant.
- Supplier access must be reviewed at least annually.
- Supplier incidents affecting BrightBox must be escalated to the operations manager and director.

## Roles and responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Director | Approves high-risk suppliers and major contracts. |
| Operations Manager | Maintains supplier records and reviews critical suppliers. |
| IT Support Provider | Advises on technical access and supplier controls. |
| Suppliers | Follow agreed security and data handling expectations. |

## Evidence or records

- supplier list
- supplier review record
- contract or terms review
- supplier access review
- supplier incident record

## Review frequency

This policy should be reviewed annually. Critical suppliers should be reviewed annually or after a significant service or security issue.

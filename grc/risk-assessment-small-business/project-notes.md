# Project Notes

This project was completed as an independent cybersecurity practice exercise. I used the BrightPath Tutoring Ltd case study to apply governance, risk and compliance concepts to a realistic small-business environment.

The work focused on defining the assessment scope, identifying key assets, assessing risks, mapping controls, and presenting the findings through written documentation and supporting visuals.

Further improvements may include expanding the control implementation plan, adding more detailed policy documentation, and comparing the risk treatment options against additional frameworks.

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the acceptable use of information systems, devices, and data belonging to or managed by Acorn Solutions Ltd. It protects the organisation, its clients, and its staff by establishing clear boundaries for the use of company resources.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and third parties who access or use company systems, networks, devices, or data — whether on company premises, remotely, or via personal devices used for work purposes.

## 3. Policy Statements

### 3.1 General Principles

All users must:
- Use company systems and data only for legitimate business purposes
- Take reasonable care to protect the systems and data they access
- Report suspected security incidents or policy violations promptly
- Complete mandatory information security awareness training

### 3.2 Device Use

- Company-issued devices must be used primarily for business purposes
- Reasonable personal use of company devices is permitted provided it does not: compromise security, consume excessive resources, or involve illegal or inappropriate content
- Users must not install unauthorised software on company devices
- Devices must be locked when left unattended (Windows + L / screen lock)
- Users must not connect company devices to unsecured or public Wi-Fi without using the company VPN

### 3.3 Internet and Email Use

- Internet access is provided for business purposes; incidental personal use is tolerated
- Users must not access, download, or distribute material that is illegal, offensive, or discriminatory
- Email must not be used to transmit confidential information to unauthorised parties
- Users must exercise caution with email attachments and links — report suspicious emails to IT Security

### 3.4 Data Handling

- Client and personal data must be handled in accordance with the Data Classification Policy
- Confidential data must not be stored on personal devices or unapproved cloud services
- Paper documents containing sensitive information must be stored securely and shredded when no longer needed

### 3.5 Social Media

- Staff must not share confidential company or client information on social media platforms
- Personal social media use must not imply company endorsement or create reputational risk

### 3.6 Remote Working

- Staff working remotely must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems
- Home networks used for work must be secured with a strong Wi-Fi password and up-to-date router firmware
- Screens must not be visible to others when processing confidential information

## 4. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

- Accessing systems or data without authorisation
- Sharing login credentials with colleagues or third parties
- Attempting to bypass security controls
- Downloading, storing, or distributing illegal content
- Using company systems to harass, threaten, or discriminate against any person
- Mining cryptocurrency or running non-business applications that consume significant resources

## 5. Monitoring

The company reserves the right to monitor use of company systems to ensure compliance with this policy and applicable law. Monitoring is conducted in accordance with applicable privacy legislation.

## 6. Consequences of Violation

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract. Serious violations may be referred to law enforcement authorities.

## 7. Review

This policy will be reviewed annually or following a significant security incident or change in the organisation's operating environment.
